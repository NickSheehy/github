As you may be aware your Mercedes‑Benz Finance Agreement is coming to an end. As a valued customer of Mercedes‑Benz Retail Group I am writing to let you know how this process works and how we can support you.

You are probably aware that there are several options available to you, these will be detailed in the communications you receive from Mercedes‑Benz Finance. Our role is to ensure you understand these options and have all of the information you need to make an informed decision.

In order to do this we would like to provide you with an End of Contract Review with one of our Specialists. This can either be done at your local Retailer or over the phone at a time that suits you. We will take you through your options and responsibilities to help you reach a decision. We will then advise Mercedes‑Benz Finance of this decision on your behalf.

Please book your Review as soon as possible. Book online today or call our booking team on 0208 232 9438 who will make the arrangements and discuss the options now that your agreement is nearly finished.

We look forward to hearing from you and seeing you soon.


Angela Shepherd
